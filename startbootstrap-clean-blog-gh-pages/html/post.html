What is Grand Bow Tag?

Probaly the best game Ever!

Grand Bow Tag is an Archery Dodgeball game. Meaning that it plays like Dodgeball, the differnce is that each player has a bow and shoots their oppentes with arrows.

Sounds cool right? We supply the bows arrows and Barricades so you don't have to wonder where you have to get the equipment from.

So why don't you give it a try? Look futrhter down for more details.

Where to Play

Just like Dodgeball you cannot play just anywhere. You need a wide open space for you, and to protect others.

What is super cool is that this game can be played both inside and out as long as there is plenty of room for a "strategic" miss.

No matter where you play, you are sure to enjoy the exhilarating feeling of letting you arrows fly. And darting out of the way of others.
